Eight disciplined modules. Nothing pictorial, nothing spare.

Every module shares a single data spine — a proposal written here is a budget there, a report later.
01 / 08

Proposal drafting

Templates mapped to Horizon Europe, ERC, COST. Deadlines appear as vertical rules on a shared calendar.

02 / 08

Budget engine

Personnel, equipment, indirect costs — all on a single ledger with automatic eligibility checks.

03 / 08

Partner network

Locate PIC-registered organisations. Manage LEAR signatures. Build consortia without spreadsheets.

Timeline & gantt

Work packages, milestones, deliverables — rendered on a baseline grid, not a cartoon bar chart.

Reporting suite
