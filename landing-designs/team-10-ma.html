Method. Not method­ology.

Three capabilities. Each one does a single thing well. There is nothing else in the product — and that is the point. What you do not see is the feature.

— 01 · 一

Deadlines, quietly kept.

Every call, every milestone, every report — held in a calendar that only speaks when it must. No dashboards that shout.

— 02 · 二

Proposals, as a single document.

Budget, workplan, partners, ethics. Co-authored in one place. Versioned. Comment threads that close themselves when resolved.

— 03 · 三

Reports that write themselves.

Spend, hours, outputs — gathered from the tools your team already uses. A draft report waits for you on the day it is due. You edit; you do not compile.
