02 — Capabilities

Eight instruments. One quiet table.

Every module was drafted against a real Horizon Europe reporting cycle. No dashboards for the sake of dashboards. No notifications that cannot be muted.

01

Timesheets, reviewed quietly

Researchers log hours against work packages in under a minute. Coordinators approve in batches. Audit trails export as signed PDF.

02

Budgets in living figures

Planned, committed, actual — shown side by side, by cost category, per partner. Forecast the overrun before the officer asks.

03

Proposals, from blank page

Structured templates for Horizon, national, and regional calls. Collaborate without ten email threads and a mystery final version.

04

Partner reporting

Collect financial statements, certificates and evidence from every consortium partner in one shared dossier. Chase once; never twice.

05

Deliverables on schedule

Every milestone mapped to owners, dates, and dependencies. Gantt on demand; a plain list when you prefer one.

06

Audit-ready exports

One click produces the binder your national contact point asks for. Figures reconciled to the cent, footnotes included.

07

GDPR & data residency

Hosted in the European Union. Processor agreements included. Single sign-on with your institution’s identity provider.

08

Correspondence, archived

Emails with project officers and partners attach themselves to the right work package. Nothing lives only in someone’s inbox.

Administration is the quiet part of research. Software should respect the quiet.

03 — Workflow

A month, as read from left to right.

GrantLume follows the rhythm of a funding period. Each step is a section heading; each section has a single owner and a single deadline.

I.

Plan the period

Draft the work plan with your consortium. Lock budget categories per partner. GrantLume carries the structure into every subsequent module.

II.

Record the work

Researchers log hours weekly against the work packages they already know. No cryptic codes; no rewiring of habits.

III.

Reconcile the books

Budgets update as invoices arrive. The overrun is visible the morning it begins, not the month after.

IV.

Report, then rest

One export produces the dossier your funder expects. Evidence, statements, and signatures, bound in order.
