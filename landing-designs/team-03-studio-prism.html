What's inside

Six tools, one calm surface, zero spreadsheets.

Built with research admins in Aarhus, Leiden, and Bologna. Every feature earns its place by removing a tab, a thread, or a weekly meeting.

Every application, every version, every reviewer — in one honest timeline.

Drag in a call, drop in co-applicants, and watch the submission tree build itself. Track Horizon, national, and foundation grants side by side.

Budgets that balance themselves.

Cost categories, overheads, currency, and partner splits — all live. If the numbers stop adding up, you find out before the reviewer does.

Deadlines that nudge, not nag.

Gentle reminders in Slack and email the week before, the day before, and the morning of. Never another 23:59 scramble.

Reports in a morning, not a week.

Interim and final reports build from the same data your team already entered. Export as PDF, DOCX, or portal-ready XML.

EU-hosted, GDPR by default.

Servers in Frankfurt and Stockholm. Role-based access. Full audit log. Your data leaves the EU only if you tell it to.

The flow

From "we got the call" to "we got funded" — in the same workspace.

GrantLume is built around the real shape of a grant: long, uneven, full of people who weren't there last time. Four steps, and none of them live in a spreadsheet.

1

Import the call

Paste the URL. We parse deadlines, eligibility, and budget caps automatically.

2

Build the team

Invite PIs, partners, finance. Everyone sees their slice; nobody sees more than they should.

3

Draft the budget

Live costing against real overhead rules. Split across work packages with a drag.

4

Report and renew

Interim reports generate from your actuals. Renewal is a button, not a quarter.
