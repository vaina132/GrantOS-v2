Volumen I An instrument for research administration MMXXVI

A considered instrument for European research funding.

GrantLume is a quiet, deliberate workspace for grant administrators and principal investigators: a single, well-ordered library in which applications, budgets, and reporting obligations are kept together, legibly, and without noise.

II. Of the Instrument's Parts

Eight modules, each considered in turn, each in its proper place.

Built with research administrators, over four years, and with the studied patience that grant work itself demands.

i.

A library of calls

Horizon Europe, ERC, national agencies, and regional instruments, kept current and well-indexed, with deadlines observed quietly and without alarm.

400+ sources · Updated daily
ii.

Budgets, legibly kept

Line items, work packages, and co-funding rules expressed in a single clean ledger that mirrors the conventions of the European programmes.

Multi-currency · Horizon templates
iii.

The reporting period

Periodic and final reports assembled from the same authoritative records that produced the proposal, avoiding the customary midnight reconstruction.

Auto-assembly · PDF, DOCX
iv.

A register of collaborators

Consortium members, rapporteurs, and external reviewers held in an orderly register, with roles, affiliations, and conflicts recorded precisely.

ORCID · ROR · GDPR-observant
v.

Deadlines, observed

A quiet calendar of cut-off dates, milestones, and review windows, drawn from your portfolio and the agencies' own published timetables.

iCal · Email · Quiet notices
vi.

A considered archive

Past applications, budgets, and reports kept together as a working archive — searchable, cross-referenced, and written, always, in plain language.

Full-text · Cross-reference
vii.

Institutional alignment

Workflows shaped to the offices that actually sign the forms, with roles for administrators, PIs, finance officers, and legal services kept distinct.

SSO · SAML · Role-by-role
viii.

European by disposition

Hosted within the Union, observant of the GDPR, and designed around the language and rhythm of European research programmes — not retrofitted.

EU-hosted · GDPR · ISO 27001
ix.

Print, bind, archive

A print stylesheet considered as carefully as the screen: every page intended to be bound, shelved, and consulted long after submission.

A4 · Letter · Signature-ready
